OUR STORY:

Los Pollos Hermanos is more than just a restaurant; it is a culinary institution that has become a cornerstone of the community. Founded by Gustavo Fring in 1986, Los Pollos Hermanos has served as a beacon of excellence in the fast-food industry, offering a unique and unforgettable dining experience.

Gustavo Fring, a self-made entrepreneur with a passion for quality, envisioned a restaurant that would exceed all expectations. Inspired by his childhood memories and the flavors of his native Chile, he sought to create a place where customers could indulge in delicious food while being treated with exceptional hospitality.

From the very beginning, Los Pollos Hermanos set itself apart from the competition. The secret to its success lies in its commitment to sourcing only the freshest ingredients and maintaining the highest standards of food preparation. Every chicken served at Los Pollos Hermanos is carefully selected, marinated in a blend of secret spices, and grilled to perfection, ensuring a mouthwatering taste that keeps customers coming back for more.
